# UAT Product Note for CHSL MVP 1.001

## Business Context
The CHSL MVP 1.001 aims to address time lag in accounting workflows across three primary roles:
1. **Society Office Clerical Staff**
2. **Backend Accounting Vendor**
3. **MC Members**

This product note emphasizes the importance of streamlined processes and user-friendly interfaces to enhance operational efficiency and reduce delays.

### Role-Specific Challenges
- **Clerical Staff** often face cumbersome processes which lead to frustration and errors.
- **Backend Accounting Vendors** require timely data entry and efficient workflows.
- **MC Members** need clarity on financial transactions and updates.

## UX/UI Focus
To ensure maximum usability for clerical staff, the following key areas are emphasized:
- **Intuitive Interface**: Designing a layout that reduces cognitive load and enhances navigational ease.
- **Idiot-Proofing**: Implementing features such as tooltips, error messages, and guided prompts to support users with varying technical abilities.

## Testing Procedures
1. **Testing Master Data**: Before proceeding to voucher creation, a thorough verification of master data is mandatory to ensure foundation accuracy.
2. **Creating Vouchers**: Test the functionality for different vendors, expense heads, and amounts to ascertain flexibility and reliability in the accounting processes.
3. **Cheque Cancellation**: Conduct trials on the cheque cancellation process to ensure it functions without glitches and follows compliance protocols.

## Evaluation Metrics
The evaluation should focus on usability from the clerical staff's perspective:
- How quickly can users navigate from one task to another?
- Are there reduction and simplification of processes compared to previous workflows?
- User feedback on clarity and error management throughout voucher creation and cancellation tasks.

## Conclusion
The UAT for CHSL MVP 1.001 is a crucial step towards achieving efficiency in accounting workflows. By focusing on usability and practical testing scenarios, the goal is to minimize time lag and enhance the overall experience for clerical staff, accounting vendors, and MC members. Success in this phase will lay the groundwork for future enhancements and iterations.
